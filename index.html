The Meaning

Hriday (हृदय) means Heart — Setu (सेतु) means Bridge. Together, we are the bridge that connects hearts through sacred celebrations, uniting families in devotion and joy.

✦

Our Philosophy

We believe every celebration is a sacred journey. Rooted in Jain values of purity, non-violence, and devotion, we create events that honor tradition while embracing modern elegance.
